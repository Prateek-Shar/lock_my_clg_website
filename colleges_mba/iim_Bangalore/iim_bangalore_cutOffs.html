Admission to IIM Bangalore in 2025 is a two-step procedure. Candidates with valid CAT 2024 scores are first selected for the Personal Interview (PI) and Writing Ability Test (WAT). To be eligible for the next round of IIM Bangalore admissions, candidates must score higher than the minimal cutoffs in both the sectional and overall percentile ranks, as well as have a positive (greater than zero) raw score in every part of the CAT 2024 exam.
