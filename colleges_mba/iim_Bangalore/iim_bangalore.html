One of Asia's top graduate schools of management is the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIMB). IIMB is an Institute of National Importance as defined by the IIM Act of 2017. About 1,200 students enrolled in various degree-granting programs, 104 full-time faculty members, and approximately 5,000 participants in Executive Education each year make up IIMB. "Let our study be enlightening" is the Sanskrit phrase that appears on our logo, तेजस्वि नावधीतमस्तु (tejasvi navadhitamastu). Our goal is to become a globally recognised academic institution that promotes excellence in entrepreneurship, management, and innovation for the benefit of society, business, and government.
