Life At Campus

A 100-acre oasis in south Bengaluru, Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIMB), with its all-stone architecture, lush verdant woods and landscaped gardens, offers an idyllic environment to engage in management studies and learning. IIMB has top-notch infrastructure that facilitates excellence in teaching, research, consulting and other professional activities. The institute is developing second campus in Jigani, about 27 km from the Bannerghatta Road campus.
The IIM-B library is located next to the amphitheatre in a separate, four-story building that is 55,000 square feet in size. Its electronic collection includes roughly 79 resources that are available both on and off campus around-the-clock. There are sixteen hostel blocks at IIM-B, one executive block, and executive housing at the Management Development Centre, or MDC for short. Standard amenities include 24-hour Internet access, fully automated laundry machines, a hair salon, a music room, a student-run library, a recreation area, and a shop on the hostel grounds. Additionally offered are guest rooms for guests, laundry and ironing facilities, and an intercom system.

IIM Bangalore Hostel

IIM Bengaluru has 16 hostel blocks, one executive block and the executive accommodation at the Management Development Center. There are separate hostels for boys and girls. Both the in-campus hostels have single occupancy.
