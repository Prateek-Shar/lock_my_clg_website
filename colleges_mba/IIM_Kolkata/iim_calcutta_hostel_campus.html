Life At Campus

The IIM Calcutta, which is situated in Joka, provides a community-like atmosphere where our professors and students may concentrate on their intellectual and personal development. The IIMC campus features top-notch instructional facilities and academic buildings that are constantly being renovated to support learning. The school has never had to make any concessions in order to build these outstanding facilities because of our devoted alumni.

Computing Facilities

The academic community's use of information technology has always been given particular weight by the institute. Around 1000 PCs, four digital Alpha servers, one IBM RS-6000 server, and seven Novell file servers are all part of the institute's network. Users can access the Internet indefinitely using VSNL over a 2MBPS and 64KBPS radio link.

Auditorium

The best auditorium in the nation is found on the campus of M Calcutta. This top-notch facility, which has 750 seats, was constructed at a cost of more than Rs 9 crores. It has a state-of-the-art, high-end lighting and sound system in addition to air conditioning. All of the important activities on the IIM C campus are held at the Audi, as we call it.

Library

In honour of Dr. B. C. Roy, the first Chairman of the Indian Institute of Management Calcutta, the library was established in March 1962 and given the name B. C. Roy Memorial Library. The main goal of the Library's design was to satisfy the demands of the Institute's academic programs. Over 1.6 lakh volumes of books and bound journals make up the Library's enormous collection. It offers access to over 40,000 online full-text journals and has 500 journal subscriptions. It is regarded as one of Asia's top management libraries. It is furnished with contemporary amenities, and the computerised library operations and services offer great ease.

Finance Lab

A cutting-edge Financial Research and Trading Lab (the Finance Lab) has been established at IIM Calcutta.A Bloomberg terminal in the lab gives users access to real-time market data, corporate news, national and international financial statistics, and state-of-the-art financial analytical tools. The only IIM with access to a Bloomberg terminal is ours. A trading exchange simulator (XSTrade) with real-time data flow from five markets—NSE-Cash and F&O, BSE-Cash, NCDEX, and MCX—is another feature of the lab.
