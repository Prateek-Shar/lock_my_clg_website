IIM Calcutta MBA is a two-year residential course offered full-time. It is also known as the Postgraduate Programme in Management (PGP). This programme has a two-tier structure: core curriculum, summer internship, and elective courses. IIM Calcutta MBA admission is done based on CAT scores. The final selection to the IIM Calcutta MBA course is based on parameters, including CAT scores, WAT-PI, academic performance, work experience, and gender diversity.
Admission to the MBA program at IIM Calcutta in 2025 is determined by the CAT Cutoff list. Admission will be available to candidates who meet both the sectional and overall CAT cutoff percentiles. The Cutoff is only seen by logging into each candidate's individual account; the institute does not make it public.
