Admission to the MBA program at IIM Calcutta in 2025 is determined by the CAT Cutoff list. Admission will be available to candidates who meet both the sectional and overall CAT cutoff percentiles.
Two cutoffs are released by IIM Calcutta: the qualifying CAT cutoff and the final CAT cutoff. Candidates must meet a minimal qualifying cutoff in order to sit for the CAT Final Exam (WAT and PI). It seems that the final CAT cutoff at IIM Calcutta is consistently higher than the qualifying cutoff.
