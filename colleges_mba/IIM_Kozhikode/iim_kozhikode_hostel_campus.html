Library

With its cutting-edge information services and contemporary collection of knowledge resources, the library plays a crucial role in supporting the intellectual endeavors of local residents, students, and faculty. 35500 print volumes, 200000 e-books, 270 print journals, 20,000 electronic journals, and 54 electronic databases are among the resources the library has to offer. A variety of information services that meet the highest professional standards are provided by the library to aid in the learning process. The IIMI Library actively participates in both the INDEST and IIM consortiums.

Hostel

The institute features 15 well-designed dorms that can house more than 910 students. Additionally, some hostels have air conditioning. To meet the students' computing demands, LAN connections have been installed in every room of the hostel. Each hostel has a television and laundry facilities. There are STD/ISD call-making facilities in every hostel. Additionally, the institute offers 116 comfortable, standard, and premium air-conditioned rooms with connected bathrooms, cable-equipped TVs, small refrigerators, and internet access for participants in management development programs.
