The Government of India and the State Government of Kerala established the institute in 1996.Â  The Indian Institute of Management Kozhikode is located on two hillocks in the Kunnamangalam Area of the historic city of Calicut, Kerala, and occupies around 112.5 acres of land. With its top-notch facilities, which include air-conditioned classrooms, guest care areas, and LAN/WAN connectivity, IIMK has one of the most beautiful and oxygen-rich campuses in the nation, allowing all of its residents to enjoy a healthy lifestyle.
One of the fastest-growing management schools in the nation, the Indian Institute of Management Kozhikode began with a batch of 42 students in 1997 for its first flagship PGP course. Since then, it has expanded significantly, graduating 1200+ students in 2024. In the 'Management' category, IIM Kozhikode has been placed third by the NIRF 2024 Rankings. The Association of AMBA and EQUIS have also granted Calicut IIM its accreditation.
