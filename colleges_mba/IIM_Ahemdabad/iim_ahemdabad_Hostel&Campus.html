Student Accomodation

With a total capacity of 740 residents, IIM-A student housing includes 25 understudy apartments. Married students can also live in single-room dorms. There are ten single rooms and a dining space on each level of the student residences. Additionally, common spaces and occasionally snack shops are located in the buildings housing the single-room dormitories. Additionally, there are single-occupancy and shared rooms for both girls and boys.
