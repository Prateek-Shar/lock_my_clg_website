Soon after the CAT 2024 results are announced, the IIM Ahmedabad CAT cutoff 2024 will be made public. Admission to IIM Ahmedabad in 2025 is a two-step procedure. The first process involves shortlisting applicants for the Analytical Writing Test (AWT) and Personal Interview (PI) based on their valid CAT 2024 scores. A candidate must have a positive (greater than zero) raw score in every part of the CAT 2024 exam, be over the minimal cutoffs in the sectional percentile ranks, and have an overall percentile rank.
