The second-oldest IIM, IIM Ahmedabad was established in 1961 and is frequently named India's top business school. NIRF 2024 placed IIM Ahmedabad at the top of the list of 100 Indian B-Schools. The cutoff score for IIM Ahmedabad's CAT shortlisting is still around the 80 percentile, and students who score more than 99 percentile have a strong chance of being called for an interview.
