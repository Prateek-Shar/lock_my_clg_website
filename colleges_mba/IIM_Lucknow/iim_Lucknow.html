Reviews

The Indian government founded the fourth IIM, IIM Lucknow, in 1984. The recently founded IIM Jammu, IIM Rohtak, and IIM Kashipur are mentored by IIM Lucknow. IIM Lucknow is well-known for its strong industry linkage, varied student body, and academic quality. In the Management Category, IIM Lucknow was ranked sixth by IIRF 2024 and seventh by NIRF 2024. Additionally, the Institute was ranked 85th in the FT Global MBA 2024 Rankings. Through international competitions and exchange programs, the institute exposes its students to a global audience. IIM Lucknow's Flagship PGP program is well-known.
