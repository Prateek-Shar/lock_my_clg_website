The IIM Lucknow MBA cutoff 2024 is determined by a number of factors, including the candidates' CAT entrance exam scores. The Qualifying CAT cutoff and the Final CAT cutoff are the two CAT cutoffs that the institute announces. The minimum requirement for IIM Lucknow to be eligible for the WAT-PI round is to meet the qualifying CAT cutoff. The qualifying cutoff is never lower than the final CAT cutoff. The qualifying CAT cutoff for MBA students in the general category for IIM Lucknow admissions in 2025 is set at the 90th percentile. At the postgraduate level, IIM Lucknow admits students to MBA, MBA (ABM), and MBA (SM) programs.
