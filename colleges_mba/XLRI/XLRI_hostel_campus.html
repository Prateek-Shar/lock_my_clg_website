Auditorium

The campus has two state-of-the-art auditoriums to accommodate large seating . Fr Tome Auditorium has a seating capacity of about 150
The Tata Auditorium, with a seating capacity of about 1500 hosts all major events

Sports and Recreation

Sports are taken seriously at XLRI. The fields/courts on campus include Badminton, Basketball, Cricket, Football, Tennis and Volleyball. There is a multi-gymnasium located in the building named after Mr. S. C. Sarkar.
XL's collaboration with local clubs and other sports associations provides students with access to a synthetic athletics track, tennis courts, squash courts, and swimming pools in addition to the campus facilities.

Student Residential Facilities

Students enrolled in residential programs at XLRI have access to hostel facilities, which include four dormitory blocks totaling more than 400 rooms. Each dorm room contains access points that connect it to the school network as well as other network services and resources.

Classrooms

Access points to the campus-wide network are available in XLRI's air-conditioned lecture halls, exam halls, computer center, and syndicate rooms. Twelve cutting-edge classrooms with capabilities for multimedia presentations are available. Each lecture hall can accommodate 70 students and is set up like an amphitheater. Closed-circuit recording and replay of lectures are also supported in these classrooms. The four Syndicate Rooms can be used for a variety of academic activities.
