Hostel

The NMIMS hostel facilities in Mumbai consists of three rented residential accommodations (managed by the university itself) and two fully functional hostels, one for each gender. Basic facilities like water purifiers, common areas, entertainment areas, TVs, laundry facilities, security, and surveillance systems are provided in every hostel.

Sports

The University has various sports facility for the students, like Cricket, Table Tennis, Badminton, Football etc. for the overall developments of students and infusing the sports spirit in the students.

Available Facilities: Basketball Court | Cricket Ground | Swimming Pool | Football | Table Tennis | Volleyball | Carrom Board | Chess

Library

The NMIMS library has air conditioning and is tastefully decorated.All facets of business and management, pharmacy and pharmacology, engineering, architecture, science, and commerce, as well as related fields like economics, behavioural science, information technology, law, etc., are covered in the library's vast collection of 58 thousand volumes of books. In addition to books and periodicals, the library also has newspapers, reports, case studies, audiovisual materials, management games, psychology and management tests, and news clippings.
