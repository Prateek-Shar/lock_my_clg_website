NMIMS University was founded in 1981 and is based in Vile Parle West, Mumbai. It also has campuses in Shirpur, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Indore, Navi Mumbai, Dhule, and Chandigarh. With 17 component schools and 9 centres, SVKM's NMIMS, which is considered the university's Mumbai campus, provides UG, PG, and other courses to students in the management, law, and other fields. In addition to being certified by NBA, ABET, AACSB, and NAAC with an A+ grade, NMIMS Mumbai has also received approval from AICTE and UGC.
NMIMS University is ranked 258 by the QS Asian University Rankings 2024 - Southern Asia category and 801+ by the QS Asian University Rankings 2024. Hence, NMIMS Mumbai is one of the top-ranked universities present in India.
