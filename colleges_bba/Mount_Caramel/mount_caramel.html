Founded in 1948, Mount Carmel College (abbreviated MCC) is situated in Bangalore, Karnataka. The NCTE and AICTE have authorized the courses offered by the private institution MCC. It provides 76 undergraduate and graduate courses. Business & Management Studies, Design, Humanities & Social Sciences, Medicine & Health Sciences, Science, IT & Software, Accounting & Commerce, Hospitality & Travel, Mass Communication & Media, and Teaching & Education are among the streams in which the institute provides these programs. The campus of Mount Carmel College is well-equipped with all the latest amenities.
