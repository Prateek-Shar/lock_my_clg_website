Hostel

The "Mother Mary Hostel" is a college dorm that spans two buildings and has refectories, TV and reading rooms, a gym, and Wi-Fi. The hostel offers efficient solid waste management and solar water heaters.

Library

There are around one lakh volumes and journals in the college's central library. Additionally, it has an e-resources subscription. Under the "National Library and Information Services Infrastructure for Scholarly Content" project, institutional members get access to around 135,000 e-books and 6000 e-journals. The library offers a reprographic facility.Forty PCs are available for internet and e-resource access at the e-library.
