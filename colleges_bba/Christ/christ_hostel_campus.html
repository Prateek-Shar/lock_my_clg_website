Library

There are 2,85,595 volumes, 1,47,755 titles, 552 magazines, and 30 newspapers in the collection of the Christ University library. The library is a member of the British Council Library, DELNET, and others. It offers the Plagiarism/Similarity Check Service (Turnitin) and Reprography.
