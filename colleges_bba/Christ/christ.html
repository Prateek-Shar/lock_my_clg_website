In 1969, "CHRIST (Deemed to be University)" was founded as "Christ College." Through its nine colleges of study, Christ University provides students with undergraduate, graduate, doctoral, and other courses. These classes are offered in a number of different fields, including management, law, and science. The NBA and NAAC have given Christ University Bangalore, Karnataka, an A+ grade for accreditation. Christ University in Bangalore, India, is well-known for its flagship programs, which include law, BTech, and MBA. Prominent figures who visit the college include Sudha Murty, the Dalai Lama, and others. The reputed university boasts more than 40 academic departments and more than 520 international students.
Christ (Deemed-to-be) University's primary campus is situated in Hosur, Bengaluru, while the other two campuses are situated in Bannerghatta and Kengari.
