One of the member institutions of Symbiosis International University (Deemed to Be) is the Symbiosis Centre for Management Studies (SCMS), located in Pune. A well-known institution in India for Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) programs, SCMS Pune was founded in 2004. The course is offered full-time and has AICTE approval.
SCMS has collaboration with 17 foreign universities for student exchange programme. Some of them are listed below:
Berlin School of Economics & Law, Germany Nanyang Technological University, Singapore University of Cork, Ireland IESEG, France etc.
