Hostel

With a capacity of 78 for girls and 88 for boys, the college offers a dorm for both sexes. Separately, there are 24 rooms in two wings for boys and girls. Every hostel has seven floors, including the lowest floor. The ground floor of the relevant hostel is where the hostel office is situated. Each room is shared by three pupils. Each hostel inmate has access to a bed and mattress, a chair and writing table, a wardrobe, and a library. Each room has a bathroom and toilet adjacent, as well as air conditioning. The service provider's housekeeping employees assist in keeping the hostel's rooms and other places clean.

Classrooms

The classrooms are equipped with Wi-Fi and air conditioning. The newest furniture is used in classrooms to provide a cosy space for learning. Every classroom includes a podium with the newest technology for audio and video. There are notice boards and white marker boards in every classroom.

Library

There are 19498 books in the library of Shaheed Sukhdev College of Business Studies. The library also has subscriptions to 13 national and business newspapers, as well as 51 publications and journals of national and international renown. The library is an institutional member of both N-LIST (National Library and Information Services Infrastructure for Scholarly Content) and DELNET (Developing Library Network). Most significantly, KOHA, an internationally recognised integrated library management system, is a fully modern web-based open source solution that has automated all of our college library's operations.
