A public institution, Shaheed Sukhdev College of Business Studies, University of Delhi, is also called Shaheed Sukhdev College of Business Studies. It was founded in Delhi in 1987. The institution offers both full-time and part-time undergraduate, graduate, and certificate programs. Ten courses in all, covering a range of specialisations, are available. The institute has 9 specialisations to offer that include Cyber Security, Data Analytics, Digital Marketing, Financial Modelling, Financial Planning & Analysis, Investment Operations, Operations. Courses offered at Shaheed Sukhdev College of Business Studies include B.Sc., BBA, PG Diploma, BBM/BMS, and Certificate.
Each member of the teaching staff has a doctorate in their subject and is extremely qualified.For students who truly need it, the college provides a variety of scholarship help options.The college's placement records are excellent.
